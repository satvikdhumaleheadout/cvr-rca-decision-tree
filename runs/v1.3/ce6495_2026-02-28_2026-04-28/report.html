Root Cause

What broke?
S2C collapsed −6.36pp (32.7% → 26.3%), accounting for the entire CVR drop. The drop is broad across all 5 major experiences (UTV Raptor −7.4pp, Movie Sites −6.5pp, Jungle Expedition −6.6pp) and all devices — not concentrated anywhere.
Why did it break?
Spring break and Easter demand progressively sold out Kualoa Ranch's near-term April inventory. Users arriving in Hawaii and opening the date picker found April dates gone — only May+ dates available. As of April 29, every active Kualoa Ranch experience shows next available date = May 2. Users who could not book for their current trip window abandoned at the select page.
When did it break?
Gradual — S2C was 38% on March 30, 22% by April 15, and 13-19% in the final week (April 22-28). No single break date; depletion compounded daily as the season filled. This is entirely structural: last year (LY) showed +5pp CVR improvement at this same calendar position.
